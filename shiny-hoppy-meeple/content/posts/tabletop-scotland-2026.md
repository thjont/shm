Ten of us from Shiny Hoppy Meeple are making the trip to [Tabletop Scotland](https://tabletopscotland.co.uk/) this year
— our biggest group outing yet.

[Tabletop Scotland](https://tabletopscotland.co.uk/) is a board game, card game, and roleplaying game convention held at
the **Royal Highland Centre**, running **4th–6th September 2026**.

## What's on

The convention has something for everyone — open play with thousands of seats, tournaments, RPG sessions, seminars, a
bring-and-buy, a games library to try before you buy, and a playtest zone for games in development. There's also a
strong exhibitor presence across all three days.

**Opening times:**

| Day | Times |
| ----- | ------- |
| Friday 4th | 12:00 – 23:00 |
| Saturday 5th | 10:00 – 23:00 |
| Sunday 6th | 10:00 – 17:00 (Main Hall) / 10:00 – 19:00 (Upstairs) |

## Join us

We'll be there as a group, so if you spot us come and say hello — or better yet, join us for a game.

Tickets are available at [tabletopscotland.co.uk](https://tabletopscotland.co.uk/).

Keep an eye on our [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/shinyhoppymeepleni/) for updates and photos from the event.
