Shiny Hoppy Meeple is a board game community in East Belfast. We're a friendly bunch who love games of all kinds — from quick card games to heavy strategy titles — and we always have room for new players.

## When and where

We meet **every Thursday from 7:30 to 11pm** at [Boundary Taproom](https://www.instagram.com/boundarytaproom/) in East Belfast.

We also organise occasional **Saturday sessions** — follow us on Instagram to find out when the next one is.

## New faces welcome

Never played with us before? No problem. Turn up, grab a drink, and we'll find a game that suits you. We don't take ourselves too seriously.

## Stay in touch

Follow us on Instagram at [@shinyhoppymeepleni](https://www.instagram.com/shinyhoppymeepleni/) for session updates, game photos, and the odd Saturday announcement.
