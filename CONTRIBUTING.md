# Contributing content

This guide covers how content on the [Shiny Hoppy Meeple website](https://shiny-hoppy-meeple.pages.dev)
is added and removed. If you want to change how the website *works* (code, layout, build), see the
[developer guide](DEVELOPMENT.md) instead.

---

## Members and game libraries

Member profiles, shadow libraries, and game overrides are managed in the **site data spreadsheet**
by maintainers. If you'd like to:

- **Add your collection** — share your BGG username or GeekList ID with a maintainer.
- **Update your details** — let a maintainer know and they'll edit the sheet.
- **Remove your profile** — ask a maintainer to remove your row from the sheet.

Changes to the sheet are picked up automatically on the next scheduled BGG data update (daily at
4 am). Your games will appear across the site after that run completes.

---

## Posts

Blog posts (news, event write-ups, etc.) are Markdown files committed directly to the repo by
maintainers. To suggest a post, open a plain GitHub Issue describing what you'd like to publish, or
send it to a maintainer directly.

---

## Questions?

Open a plain GitHub Issue or ask a maintainer. We're happy to help.
